Rice

Description

The key to cooking rice like a proper Asian is to first have an Asian rice cooker.

  • Rice: 2 Cups
  • Water: 2 Cups
  • Olive Oil: a drizzle
  1. Rinse rice underwater
  2. Add water and olive oil to rice, then let it sit for 10 minutes
  3. Use a translator to make sure the rice cooker is on the right setting for white rice
  4. Wait for the rice to finish (~40 minutes)
  5. Agitate rice to let out excess moisture, then let it sit for 10 minutes
